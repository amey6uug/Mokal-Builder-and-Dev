Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Browse through our most commonly asked questions

What services does Mokal Builder offer? +
We offer comprehensive construction services including Residential Construction, Commercial Construction, Renovation & Remodeling, Interior Design, Project Management, Real Estate Development, and Land Development. Each service is customized to meet your specific requirements with premium quality standards.
How long has Mokal Builder been in business? +
Mokal Builder was founded in 2008 and has over 15 years of experience in the construction industry. We've successfully completed numerous residential, commercial, and real estate development projects across Mumbai and surrounding areas.
What areas do you serve? +
Our primary service area is Mumbai and the greater Mumbai region. However, we also undertake large-scale projects in other major cities across Maharashtra. Contact us to discuss your project location.
What types of residential projects do you handle? +
We specialize in luxury apartments, independent villas, residential complexes, townhouses, and custom homes. Our residential projects range from 2BHK apartments to large luxury villas with premium amenities.
Do you provide design services for residential projects? +
Yes! We have in-house architects and interior designers who provide complete design services including architectural planning, 3D visualization, interior design, and landscape design. We work closely with you to bring your vision to life.
What is included in residential construction? +
Our residential construction includes site preparation, foundation work, structural work, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, flooring, painting, woodwork, and all finishing work. We also handle government approvals and provide a 10-year structural warranty.
What commercial projects do you undertake? +
We handle office towers, retail spaces, shopping malls, corporate campuses, hotels, hospitals, educational institutions, and mixed-use developments. Our commercial expertise spans from small office renovations to large-scale commercial complexes.
Can you work with existing tenants during renovation? +
Yes, we specialize in occupied building renovations with minimal disruption. We work in phases, schedule work during off-hours, and maintain strict safety protocols to ensure your business operations continue smoothly.

Do you provide free estimates? +
Yes, we provide free initial consultations and estimates. Our team will visit your site, understand your requirements, and provide a detailed quotation with transparent cost breakdowns. Use our online estimator for instant approximate costs.
What payment terms do you offer? +
We typically work on a milestone-based payment structure: 10% advance, 30% on completion of foundation, 30% on structural completion, 20% on finishing work, and 10% on final handover. Terms can be customized based on project requirements.
How long does a typical residential project take? +
A standard residential home (2000-3000 sq ft) takes approximately 12-18 months from groundbreaking to completion. Smaller projects may take 8-10 months, while luxury villas can take 18-24 months. Timeline depends on design complexity, approvals, and weather conditions.
What factors can delay a construction project? +
Common delays include: government approval delays, monsoon season, design changes, material shortages, site access issues, and unforeseen ground conditions. We build buffer time into our schedules and maintain transparent communication about any potential delays.
Can I track my project progress? +
Absolutely! We provide regular project updates through site visits, progress photos, video walkthroughs, and detailed reports. Our project managers are available for consultations, and you can visit the site at scheduled times to monitor progress firsthand.
Do you handle government approvals and permits? +
Yes, we handle all necessary government approvals, building permits, NOCs, and legal documentation. Our team is experienced with local regulations and works with municipal authorities to ensure full compliance.
What warranty do you provide? +
We provide a 10-year structural warranty on all our construction projects. Additionally, we offer 1-year warranty on finishing work and fittings, and 2-year warranty on electrical and plumbing systems. Extended warranties are available for premium materials.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes, Mokal Builder is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance, workers' compensation, and project insurance. All our certifications and licenses are available for review.
How do I get started with my project? +
Getting started is easy! Contact us through our website, phone, or WhatsApp. Schedule a free consultation where we discuss your vision, budget, and timeline. We'll conduct a site visit, prepare a proposal, and guide you through every step of the process.
