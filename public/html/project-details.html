Project Overview

The Luxury Apartment Complex represents the pinnacle of modern residential living in Pune. Spanning across 5 acres of prime real estate, this project features 200 meticulously designed units that blend contemporary architecture with sustainable building practices.

Each apartment is equipped with smart home technology, allowing residents to control lighting, temperature, and security systems through their smartphones. The complex boasts premium finishes, Italian marble flooring, modular kitchens, and floor-to-ceiling windows that offer breathtaking city views.

Key Features

  • Smart home automation system in every unit
  • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance
  • Clubhouse with gym, swimming pool, and recreational facilities
  • Landscaped gardens and children's play area
  • Multi-level parking with EV charging stations
  • Rainwater harvesting and solar panels
  • High-speed elevators and power backup
  • Proximity to schools, hospitals, and shopping centers

Construction Excellence

Built using RCC framed structure with earthquake-resistant design, the complex meets all international safety standards. We employed eco-friendly construction methods, including the use of fly ash bricks, energy-efficient windows, and a comprehensive waste management system during construction.

The project was completed ahead of schedule with zero safety incidents, demonstrating our commitment to both quality and worker welfare. Our team of experienced engineers and architects worked closely to ensure every detail met our exacting standards.

Sustainability Features

Environmental responsibility is at the core of this project. The complex features:

  • Solar panels generating 40% of common area electricity
  • Rainwater harvesting system with 500,000-liter capacity
  • Sewage treatment plant for water recycling
  • LED lighting throughout common areas
  • Native plant landscaping requiring minimal irrigation
  • Waste segregation and composting facilities
