Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

At Mokal Builder, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Important: By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out contact forms on our website
  • Request a quote or estimate
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or WhatsApp
  • Register for an account (if applicable)

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Project details and requirements
  • Budget information
  • Any other information you choose to provide

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses
  • Device identifiers

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve website performance
  • Provide personalized content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To respond to your inquiries, provide quotes, and deliver our construction services
  • Communication: To send you project updates, appointment confirmations, and important notifications
  • Marketing: To send you newsletters, promotional materials, and information about our services (with your consent)
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Customer Support: To provide customer service and support

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal proceedings or court orders

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against:

  • Unauthorized access

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
